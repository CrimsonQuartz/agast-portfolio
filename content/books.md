## How to sync across devices
### Upload PDF and EPUB files to your library
You can read PDF and EPUB documents with Google Play Books. When you upload PDF and EPUB files to Play Books, you can read them on any device you sign in to.

You can also use bookmarks, highlights, and notes with PDF and EPUB documents across your devices. These functions work with PDF documents uploaded after January 20, 2026.

### Upload PDF & EPUB files

> 1.  ​On your computer, go to your [Play Books library](https://play.google.com/books).
> 2.  From the left, click **Your books**.
> 3.  At the top, click **Upload files**.
> 4.  Find the .pdf or .epub file you want to upload.
> 5.  Click **Select**.

To find a file you uploaded, go to your [Uploads library](https://play.google.com/books/uploads).

From there, you can download the google play books app on mobile devices,
and sign into the same google account that you used to upload to sync books, notes and progress across devices
